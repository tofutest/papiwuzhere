She smiled at me. I am certain of it. There is a quality to that expression — knowing, patient, slightly smug — that I recognized immediately as a fellow creature who has gotten away with something. We understood each other at once.

I was fully aware that this was not an original composition. Leonardo had arrived first by several centuries, and I made no attempt to conceal the influence. Great artists borrow. Great dogs copy directly.

I approached the canvas with the gravity this subject deserved. My contribution is subtle but transformative warmth. I do not claim authorship. I claim improvement.

You're welcome, Leonardo.
